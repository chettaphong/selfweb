Help & Instructions

Quick Start

  • Select a Feed: Use the dropdown menu to pick a news source.
  • Read: Click an article title in the left sidebar to read it.
  • Preview: Click "Preview in App" to see the original website without leaving.
  • Refresh: Click "Refresh All" to update all feeds in the background.

Key Features

  • Smart Date Filter: By default, it shows news from the last 3 days. If a feed has no news in that period, it automatically expands to check the last 90 days.
  • Deep Scan: When checked, the reader will scan up to 10 pages of a feed to find older articles. Uncheck this for faster loading of just the latest headlines.
  • Cached Mode: Your feeds are saved in the browser. Next time you open the file, your news loads instantly while fresh data updates in the background.

Managing Feeds

  • Click the ⚙️ Feeds button to add your own RSS URLs.
  • You can Export your list to a JSON file to save it or move it to another computer.

Troubleshooting

  • "Network Error": Some sites block access. We use a proxy to bypass this, but occasionally it may fail. Try refreshing.
  • "Storage Full": Browsers limit storage to ~5MB. If you hit this limit, the oldest articles will be automatically removed.
