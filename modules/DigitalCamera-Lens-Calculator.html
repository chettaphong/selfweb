Understanding the Comparison

Variable Optics

Focal length and Aperture (f-stop) ranges are multiplied by the crop factor. If 'End' values are empty, the lens is treated as a Prime or Constant Aperture lens.

DoF Equivalence

The 'Equivalent Aperture' indicates the background blur potential. For example, f/2.8 on APS-C looks like f/4.2 on Full Frame in terms of depth of field.

Resolution Loss

Using a smaller sensor's effective area on a larger sensor significantly reduces resolution (scales by crop factor squared).
