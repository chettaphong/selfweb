Understanding the Comparison

Optics

Focal length and Aperture are multiplied by the crop factor to find the 35mm equivalent field-of-view and background blur (DoF).

Resolution

When using a crop-sensor lens on a larger sensor, resolution is reduced by the square of the crop factor (e.g., 1.5x crop = 2.25x pixel loss).

Equivalence

Exposure (light per area) stays the same. Total light gathered (Noise performance) scales with sensor size.
